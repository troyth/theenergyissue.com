The Energy Issue works to make energy a cultural issue. Energy is as much a question of lifestyle, social values and communication as economics, policy and science, yet the former are often overlooked in discussions of power. The Energy Issue seeks to connect energy facts with energy values by reforming, discovering and advocating for energy as an issue in architecture, urbanism, film, literature, fashion, music, art, design and other forms of cultural production.
